Yucheng Jin 金昱成

Dr. Yucheng Jin is a Research Assistant Professor affiliated with the HCI-RecSys Group led by Dr. Li Chen at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU). Before joining HKBU, he was a senior UX designer at Lenovo Research. He obtained his Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Dr. Katrien Verbert and Dr. Nava Tintarev from the HCI Augment Group at the University of Leuven (KU Leuven). He holds a master's degree in computer science from the Technical University of Munich (TUM) and a bachelor's degree in information management from Wuhan University. He has published more than 20 papers in the leading conferences and journals of Human-Computer Interaction and Artificial Intelligence (e.g., CHI, CSCW, IUI, RecSys, CIKM, UMUAI) held more than ten patents granted in China and the US. He regularly serves as the program committee member for ACM RecSys and ACM IUI conferences.

Research Interests

His research is situated in the long-standing quest to "Augmenting human intellect". He does research at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Artificial Intelligence with a focus on applications in health, wellbeing, augmented creativity, and music. Currently, he is researching the following topics

  • Human-AI interaction
  • User experience of recommender systems (e.g., trust, explanation, personalization, and user control)
  • Music and augmented creativity
  • Human health and wellbeing
